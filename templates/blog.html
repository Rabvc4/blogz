Welcome to Pretty Picky

My adventures living with the world's pickiest wife.

My wife is severly introverted, her sentences short; her words few. Luckily for me, when she does speak she chooses to be either insightful or brutally honest. She is also a very picky eater who subsists a diet largely consisting of chicken. Needless to say she's a cheap date; she doesn't like steak and she won't touch seafood (She'll never order the "Surf and Turf"). The purpose of this blog is to entertain readers with either her brutal reviews or my missory.

Dewey's Pizza

If it isn't Dewey's Don't Bother

My wife loves Dewey's... so much so that when either of us is feeling pizza I know what to expect. Don't get me wrong, this isn't a complaint, Dewey's is very good. The problem is that our closest Dewey's is 26 minutes away, on a good day with no traffic. This is much too far to ever bring a pizza home too. According to my wife the pizza will always be cold and it won't be nearly as good heated up again in the oven.
